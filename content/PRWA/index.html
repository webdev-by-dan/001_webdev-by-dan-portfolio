About PRWA

In 1994, a small group of concerned citizens formed the Pequabuck River Watershed Association (PRWA) to preserve and protect the Pequabuck River and its watershed. Over the years, PRWA has advanced watershed planning, community education, monitoring, and bio-improvement projects.

We are a non-profit, 501(c)(3) organization dedicated to creating a healthy Pequabuck River—advocating to land use boards, conducting river cleanup events, monitoring water quality, and more.
