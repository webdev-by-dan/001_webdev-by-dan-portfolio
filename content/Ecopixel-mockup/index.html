What We Provide

Every Ecopixel website is tailored to your organization—but we start from a proven foundation of performance, accessibility, and long-term support.

A Complete Service

We take care of everything a successful site needs, from design and development, to an easy-to-edit CMS, to secure hosting and backups, to long-term maintenance. And we've been doing it since 2002!

Accessible Design

By focusing on the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) version 2.1 level AA, we’ll develop a website that helps you comply with the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA).

We’re Here to Help

We provide training at no cost — whenever new editors come on board, or existing editors would like a refresher. Need a helping hand? You can just email, chat, or call us — we’ll help you out.
